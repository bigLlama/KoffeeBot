KoffeeBot Commands

Economy

  • /balance - Check your balance
  • /inventory - Displays your inventory
  • /daily - Claim your daily reward
  • /work - Work to earn some money
  • /joblist - View the current available jobs
  • /job - Apply for a job and start working
  • /resign - Work to earn some money
  • /beg - Beg for money
  • /lucky - Obtain money if you are lucky
  • /withdraw - Withdraw money from the bank
  • /deposit - Deposit money into your bank account
  • /give - Give money or items to other members
  • /shop - Open the KoffeeBot shop
  • /buy - Buy an item from the shop
  • /sell - Sell an item from your inventory
  • /steal - Attempt to steal from someone
  • /recipes - Shows all craftable items
  • /craft - Craft an item
  • /slots - Risk your money in the slot machine
  • /leaderboard - View the top 10 richest KoffeeBot users

Moderation

  • /clear - Deletes a specified amount of chat messages
  • /addrole - Give someone a role from your server
  • /removerole - Remove a role from someone
  • /addnote - Create notes about a server member
  • /editnote - Edit an existing member's notes
  • /notes - View a server member's notes
  • /inspect - Inspect a server member

Fun

  • /roast - Roast someone
  • /simp - How much of a simp are you?
  • /dumb - Check your intelligence
  • /laugh - Koffeebot will laugh awkwardly
  • /meme - Enjoy some of the hottest memes from reddit
  • /o - Replace all vowels with the letter 'o'
  • /curse - Curse someone with the mildest of inconveniences
  • /topic - Starts a topic for you to chat about

Games

  • /rps - Play rock-paper-scissors with KoffeeBot
  • /bowl - Enjoy some bowling
  • /coinflip - Heads or Tails?
  • /duel - Duel with another member

Image Manipulation

  • /wanted - View a user's profile picture
  • /rip - View your tombstone
  • /punch - Punch someone
  • /dance - Enjoy some cool dance moves
  • /slap - Slap someone
  • /hug - Hug someone
  • /ship - See you chances of shipping with someone
  • /evil - Are you really that evil?
  • /shoot - Shoot another member
  • /angry - Let all that rage out
  • /blackmail - Make sure this person stays quiet

Miscellaneous

  • /greet - KoffeeBot introduces itself
  • /poll - Create a poll
  • /avatar - View a user's profile picture
  • /invite - Add KoffeeBot to other servers
  • /support - Have any problems with KoffeeBot?
  • /servers - Check how many servers are using KoffeeBot
  • /giveaway - Start a giveaway in your server
